Join the Project

PixelHearts is currently a passion project being built by people who simply love games, creativity, and internet culture.

Right now there is no funding and no pay. Everyone involved is here because they want to build something cool, meet creative people, and be part of a community driven project.

If the project grows, becomes playable, or eventually makes money, the goal is to share success with the people who helped build it.

The Honest Part

This is not a studio with investors. This is not a corporate project. This is not a job posting.

This is a group of people building something fun together.

If you're here for passion, learning, experimenting, and creating something nostalgic and new, you’re exactly who we want.

The Vision

I’m not a coder or animator. I'm a creative with a vision.

The vision is simple: bring back the feeling the internet used to have.

When places like Gaia Online, RuneScape, MySpace, and old web communities felt alive.

PixelHearts is about bringing community and gaming back together again.
