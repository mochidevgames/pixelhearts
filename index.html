Our Vision

Project: Pixel Hearts is a creative sandbox social grid, inspired by Second Life, Gaia Online, IMVU, and old Myspace.

We aim to merge community, creativity, and exploration in a modern, user-friendly virtual space.

Features

Custom avatars, social profiles, interactive spaces, and a flexible world grid designed for collaboration and fun.

World Grid

Region Description
Central Plaza Main social hub where players meet and interact
Marketplace Trade, shops, and creative economy
Clubs Music venues and community gathering places
Creator Spaces Workshops, studios, and collaborative building areas
Housing Districts Personal homes and customizable living spaces
Event Regions Spaces for events, competitions, and activities
