PixelHearts

A modern, inviting social MMO with luxury pixel vibes. Express yourself, meet friends, and explore a world inspired by classic MMOs with a shiny, glowing new aesthetic.

Our Vision

Bring back the feeling of classic online communities. Express yourself with avatars, meet friends, decorate spaces, and shape a living digital world. PixelHearts is for the creators, dreamers, and those who love social MMOs.

Core Features

  • Deep avatar customization
  • Social hangouts & player housing
  • Events, trading, and community-driven content
  • Modern, glowing retro style

Join The Project

PixelHearts is a passion project — we’re looking for coders, artists, animators, designers, and community builders to help bring this world to life. Volunteer-driven, but if the game grows we hope to reward contributors.
