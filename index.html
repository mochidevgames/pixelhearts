The Vision

Pixel Hearts ♥ is an experimental passion project exploring what the next generation of virtual social worlds could become.

Inspired by classic internet communities and modern virtual worlds, the goal is to build something creative, social, expressive, and community driven.

This project is early. There are no promises, timelines, or guarantees. Only curiosity, passion, and the desire to build something meaningful together.

What PixelHearts Is

Pixel Hearts ♥ is planned as a browser based Grid Viewer connecting users to shared virtual spaces. A dedicated viewer will be looked at in the future as we develop.

Instead of a traditional MMO, the world is made of regions connected together in a grid.

### World Grid Examples | Region | Description | |--------|-------------| | **Central Plaza** | Main social hub where players meet and interact | | **Marketplace** | Trade, shops, and creative economy | | **Clubs** | Music venues, events, and community hangouts | | **Creator Spaces** | Workshops, studios, and collaborative building areas | | **Housing Districts** | Personal homes, apartments, and customizable areas | | **Event Regions** | Spaces for scheduled events, mini‑games, and competitions |

The project will begin small with a single space and grows outward as contributors and ideas expand the platform.

Core Direction

The long-term vision explores modern systems similar to those seen in existing virtual worlds while pushing customization and creativity further.

  • Full 3D camera control
  • Walking avatars and movement
  • Advanced avatar customization
  • Attachment systems
  • Lighting and atmosphere
  • Animated objects and characters
  • Interactive environments

Creators should feel unrestricted and empowered to experiment.

Long Term Exploration

If the project evolves, Pixel Hearts ♥ could expand into a much larger platform supporting creators, events, and community driven worlds.

  • Creator tools
  • User worlds
  • Events and social hubs
  • Marketplace systems
  • Expanded avatar technology

The goal is to explore what a modern grid-based virtual world could become.
