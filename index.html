PixelHearts

A Social MMO Built for Creativity, Identity, and Community

Our Vision

PixelHearts is a living online world where players create their identity, express their style, and build communities together. Inspired by classic internet social worlds, PixelHearts modernizes the experience with deeper customization, vibrant spaces, and a welcoming atmosphere.

Our goal is to bring back the magic of online hangout games while giving players the tools to shape their own stories, fashion, and culture.

Core Pillars

Expression

Create a unique avatar with deep customization, fashion, and collectibles.

Community

Meet new people, hang out in social spaces, and form lasting friendships.

Creativity

Design rooms, showcase outfits, and help shape the culture of the world.

Economy

Earn, trade, and collect rare cosmetics and items.

What We're Building

• Deep avatar customization • Player housing and social hubs • Community events • Fashion driven economy • A vibrant evolving world
