PixelHearts

A nostalgic social MMO where creativity, community, and fun collide. Build, hang out, and play in a pixel world inspired by Gaia Online, IMVU, RuneScape, and old internet vibes.

Our Vision

Bring back the feeling of classic online communities. A place where you can express yourself with your avatar, meet friends, and shape a living digital world. PixelHearts is for the creators, the dreamers, and the ones who remember the magic of old-school social games.

Core Features

  • Deep avatar customization
  • Social hangouts & player housing
  • Events, trading, and community-driven content
  • Pixel-style art and nostalgic vibe

Join The Project

PixelHearts is a passion project — we’re looking for coders, artists, animators, designers, and community builders who want to help bring this world to life.

Currently this is a volunteer project, but if the game grows we hope to reward contributors.
