PixelHearts

A new social MMO where identity, creativity, and internet culture collide.

Our Vision

The internet used to feel like a world. PixelHearts is our attempt to bring that feeling back. A place where players can express themselves through avatars, fashion, homes, and communities. Not just a game — a digital universe shaped by the people inside it.

Core Pillars

Expression

Create unique avatars with deep customization and collectible fashion.

Community

Meet new friends, join scenes, and hang out in social spaces.

Creativity

Decorate spaces, show off style, and influence the culture.

Economy

Trade items, collect rare cosmetics, and build value.

Join The Project

PixelHearts is looking for passionate creators who want to build something fun together.
