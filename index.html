PixelHearts

A nostalgic social MMO where creativity, community, and fun collide. Build, hang out, and play in a pixel world inspired by Gaia Online, IMVU, RuneScape, and old internet vibes.

Our Vision

Bring back the feeling of classic online communities. Express yourself with avatars, meet friends, decorate spaces, and shape a living digital world. PixelHearts is for the creators, dreamers, and those who remember the magic of old-school MMOs.

Core Features

  • Deep avatar customization
  • Social hangouts & player housing
  • Events, trading, and community-driven content
  • Pixel-style art and nostalgic vibe

Join The Project

PixelHearts is a passion project — we’re looking for coders, artists, animators, designers, and community builders who want to help bring this world to life. Volunteer-driven, but if the game grows we hope to reward contributors.
